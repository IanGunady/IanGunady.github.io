Hey there! I am a Junior at Stanford University studying Mechanical Engineering. With an open mind and a humble heart, I see myself as a pursuer of truth, student of the physical world, and explorer of the engineering discipline. I am a believer in the scientific method and spend more time trying to understand and appreciate the problem before attempting to find a solution. In my eyes, this process, the mistakes made, and the lessons learned hold value equal to the results. Ultimately, I hope to genuinely care about my pursuits, wherever they may lead me.

I love cars, the beach, and especially my dog. I find the greatest pleasure lost on the open road, immersed in a board game, or wrapped in a book. Most of all, I enjoy good company and deeply value those around me; they are where I come from and made me who I am today.

Today, and every day, I wish you the best,
